How much time do you spend with your family or the ones you love?

Well if the answer is "not much" or "I am busy" etc.., then you should take a break from whatever you are doing such as your job and go sit with the people you love.
Talk with them about life and topics you enjoy, laugh, eat and have fun.Don't talk about problems but try to relax and have a good time for a moment.
